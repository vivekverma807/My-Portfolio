Introduction To coaching


Welcome To Coaching Program !

Definition of Coaching:

  • Guidance, support for personal development.
  • Facilitates goal attainment, skill enhancement.

Role of the Coach:

  • Mentor, motivator, and accountability partner.
  • Provides feedback, encouragement, and guidance.

Benefits of Coaching:

  • Improved performance, productivity, and effectiveness.
  • Enhances self-awareness, confidence, and resilience.

Coaching Techniques:

  • Active listening, questioning, and empathy.
  • Goal setting, action planning, and feedback.
  • Encouraging reflection, growth, and learning.

Types of Coaching:

  • Executive, career, and life coaching.
  • Sports, performance, and wellness coaching.
  • Leadership, team, and skill-specific coaching.

Impact of Coaching:

  • Personal growth, development, and empowerment.
  • Positive change, achievement of goals.
  • Building strong relationships, trust, and rapport.
