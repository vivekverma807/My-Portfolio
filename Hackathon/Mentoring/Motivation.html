Introduction To Motivation


Welcome To Motivation Program !

Definition and Importance of Motivation:

  • Drive directing behavior toward goals.
  • Crucial for personal, professional success.

Types of Motivation:

  • Intrinsic, extrinsic, positive, negative..
  • Internal, external factors influencing behavior

Factors Affecting Motivation:

  • Individual traits, values, beliefs.
  • Environment, work conditions, social influences.

Motivational Theories:

  • Maslow, Herzberg, Expectancy theories.
  • Needs hierarchy, hygiene factors, performance-outcome.

Motivation Strategies and Techniques:

  • Goal setting, rewards, recognition.
  • Supportive environment, communication, skill development.

Motivation Challenges and Solutions:

  • Addressing demotivating factors, individual differences.
  • Adaptation, assessment, continuous improvement.
